# TICKET-01: Basic Calculator

**Priority:** Must Have  
**Story Points:** 3  
**Points:** 4  
**Due:** Monday Week 1, End of Class

---

## 📝 USER STORY

```
As a user,
I want to perform basic arithmetic operations,
So that I can calculate results quickly.
```

---

## ✅ ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

- [ ] Create two variables with numeric values
- [ ] Perform addition on the two numbers
- [ ] Perform subtraction on the two numbers
- [ ] Perform multiplication on the two numbers
- [ ] Perform division on the two numbers
- [ ] Display all results with clear labels

---

## 📋 REQUIREMENTS

1. **Variables:**
   - Declare `num1` and `num2` with any numbers
   
2. **Operations:**
   - Calculate and store: sum, difference, product, quotient
   
3. **Output:**
   - Log each result with a descriptive label
   - Example: "Addition: 5 + 3 = 8"

---

## 💻 EXAMPLE OUTPUT

```
Calculator Results:
Addition: 10 + 5 = 15
Subtraction: 10 - 5 = 5
Multiplication: 10 * 5 = 50
Division: 10 / 5 = 2
```

---

## 🧪 TESTING

Test with different number pairs:
- Positive numbers
- Negative numbers
- Decimals
- Zero (watch out for division by zero!)
